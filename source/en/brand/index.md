## Usage Guidelines

- Do not modify logo proportions or colors
- Use the dark-background logo on dark backgrounds, transparent version on light backgrounds
- Maintain a minimum clear space of 10% of the logo size
- For other formats or sizes, open an Issue on the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/Linxira-OS/linxira-artwork)
